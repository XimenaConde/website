About Me

Ximena Conde is Wisconsin Public Radio’s Second Century News Fellow. Based out of Milwaukee, she works as a general assignment reporter covering southeastern Wisconsin. She got her start in news as Rhode Island Public Radio’s Morning Edition producer. She grew up in Queens and graduated from Rutgers University.
